Restaurant table booking bot

Apple House restaurant in Lisbon, Portugal has created a Telegram bot to help English-speaking community members book a place in seconds for their board games, quizzes, and karaoke events.

What can the bot do?

  • To see list of the upcoming  events (exported from google calendar of the restaurant)

  • To see description of the events

  • Book the table for the exact time

  • To book place on the event

  • To book table on the event 

  • To see all bookings

  • To cancel any of the booking

  • on board games days to book place on the specific game
